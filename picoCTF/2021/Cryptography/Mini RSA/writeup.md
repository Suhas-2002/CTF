For RSA Dcryption we know the realtion:
**ct = M ^ e (% n)**

On changing this : **M^e = tN + ct**

Here t is a integer.

It's given that (M ** e) is just barely larger than N. So, we can get the message on trying t with 1,2..., untill we find a match.
